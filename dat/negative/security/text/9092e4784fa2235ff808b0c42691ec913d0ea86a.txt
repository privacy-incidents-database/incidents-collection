September 6, 1975, Page 26 The New York Times Archives
ROCHESTER, N.Y., Sept. 5Security precautions for Vice President Rockefeller were considerably tightened here today after he received word of the assassination attempt on Mr. Ford.During a fiveandahalfhour visit here, Mr. Rockefeller dedicated the new Park Ridge Hospital and a family medicine center at Highland Hospital and attended a reception with local Republicans. He also held a news conference. It was his first visit since 1972 to Rochester, where he was nominated for Governor in 1958.
