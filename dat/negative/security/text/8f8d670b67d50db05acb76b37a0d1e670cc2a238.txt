October 17, 1975, Page 70 The New York Times Archives
DETROIT, Oct. 16 (UPI)President Ford's limousine was flown to suburban Livonia today for repairs in a Ford Motor Company body shop. A spokesman for the auto maker said that the $500,000 Lincoln Continental would be repaired by specialists and flown black to Washington as soon as possible.
