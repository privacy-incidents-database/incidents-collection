October 17, 1975, Page 70 The New York Times Archives
MERIDEN, Conn., Oct. 16 (AP)The Hartford police said today the results of a blood test, Mr. Salamites took Tuesday were negative. His blood contained five onehundredths of 1 per cent alcohol, and a person is considered legally under the influence of alcohol only when the level reaches fifteen onehundredths of 1 per cent, the police said.
