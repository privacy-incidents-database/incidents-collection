September 6, 1975, Page 26 The New York Times Archives
WASHINGTON, Sept. 5Larry M. Buendorf, the Secret Service agent who disarmed the woman allegedly trying to shoot President Ford today, is a former Navy flier who joined the Secret Service in 1970Mr. Buendorf, who is 37 years old, was born in Wells, Minn., and attended Mankato State College in nearby Mankato, about 80 miles southwest of Minneapolis. He was graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree, having majored in business administration, in 1959. He was on the college football team.He served as a Navy flier from 1959 to 1963, and then in the Naval Investigative Service. He joined the Secret Service in 1970, working first in the Chicago field office. He has been a member of the Presidential Protection Division, the White House Detail, since 1972.
