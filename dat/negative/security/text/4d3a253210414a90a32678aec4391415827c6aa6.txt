December 14, 1975, Page 63 The New York Times Archives
ATLANTA, Dec. 13 (AP)The Presidential candidate of the Socialist Workers Party has accused the city of Atlanta of harassment after his acquittal of charges of disorderly conduct and criminal trespass. The charges against Peter Camejo were lodged after he was arrested Feb. 3 outside a downtown hotel where President Ford was speaking. City Solicitor Mary. Welcome asked that the charges be dropped Thursday, offering no explanation.
