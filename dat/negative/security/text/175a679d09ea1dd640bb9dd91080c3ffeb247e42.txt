October 19, 1975, Page 22 The New York Times Archives
RENO, Oct. 18 (AP)A Federal judge has ordered the release of a New Jersey man charged with threatening the life of President Ford because more than four and a half months had elapsed between his arrest and arraignment. Charges were dismissed against Charles Valentine Van Giezen 33 years old, on the ground that his constitutional right to a speedy trial had been violated.
