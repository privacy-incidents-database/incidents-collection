WASHINGTON, Oct. 19 (UPI) Treasury Secretary William E. Simon said today that there was no evidence of Secret Service negligence in protecting President Ford in recent gun incidents rnd an auto crash. But he said on the A.B.C. television program Issues and Answers that the Treasury Department, of which the Secret Service is a port was working to upgrade our performance in this area. He was Interviewed on ABCTV's Issues and Answers program.
