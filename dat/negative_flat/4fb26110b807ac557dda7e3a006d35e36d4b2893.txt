Archives|2d Threat on President Returns Man to Prison
October 9, 1975, Page 27  Archives
SACRAMENTO, Calif., Oct. 8 (AP)A man who served five years for threatening to kill President Nixon got another fiveyear term today for threatening the life of President Ford on Aug. 15.Thomas D. Elbert, 35 years old, of Louisville, Ky., was sentenced by a Federal District court judge, Thomas J. McBride, who is handling the case of Lynette Fromme.Mr. Elbert had been out of prison only three months when he telephoned the Secret Service office here and said, I'm going to kill your boss, Ford, Secret Service agents said.
