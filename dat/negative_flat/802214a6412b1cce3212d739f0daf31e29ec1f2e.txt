Archives|Secret Service Confirms A 2d Hartford Collision
October 29, 1975, Page 29  Archives
WASHINGTON, Oct. 28 (AP)The Secret Service has confirmed that one of its cars bumped President Ford's limousine shortly after the Ford car was in a collision at a Hartford, Conn., intersection on Oct. 14.The right front fender was damaged in the original collision. The President was not hurt. Special Secret Service agent, Lewis McClam, said today that a minute later, as the motorcade pulled away, the Secret Service car trailing the limousine hit the bumper of the Presidential car and broke a tail light.It was so slight we didn't consider it an accident, Mr. McClam said.
