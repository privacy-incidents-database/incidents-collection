Bob Mackie gowns, Cartier baubles and a jeweled study of a Lily of the Valley leaf by Faberg that filled the comedian Joan Riverss Manhattan penthouse are coming to auction at Christies.With 200 lots in a live sale on June 22 and about 80 lots in an online auction June 1623, estimates will range from $500 to $200,000. Christies previously sold the contents of the Connecticut home of Ms. Rivers, who died in 2014.The sale offers a through-the-keyhole experience for fans across the globe, Melissa Rivers, the comedians daughter, said in a statement. The auction celebrates her elegance and collecting style. There is something to suit every taste and budget.A portion of the proceeds will go toward Gods Love We Deliver, which provides meals to people in need in the New York area, and to Guide Dogs for the Blind.
